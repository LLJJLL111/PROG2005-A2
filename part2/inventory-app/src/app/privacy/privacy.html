Privacy & Security Policy

Data Storage

All inventory data (product details, supplier information, stock levels, and notes) is stored locally on your device's browser storage. No data is transmitted to external servers, cloud platforms, or third-party services.

Data Access

Your inventory data is only accessible through this application on the device where it was created. No other users, devices, or systems can access your data unless you manually export or share it.

Data Retention

Your data remains stored on your device until you clear your browser's local storage or uninstall the application. We do not collect, monitor, or retain any of your data on external systems.

Security Measures

  • Local storage encryption for sensitive inventory data
  • No user data collection or tracking
  • No cookies or analytics tools used
  • Offline functionality - no internet connection required

Note: For maximum security, we recommend regularly backing up your browser data or exporting important inventory records to a local file.
